HalApp

What We Do

Our application connects farmers (customers) in need of farming equipment with drivers (who are also farmers) who own the required equipment, providing an end-to-end service without the need for renting.

Our Mission

To empower land holders to be able to do farming from anywhere in the world with a click on thier cell phone.
